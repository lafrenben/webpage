Ben Lafreniere


Research Science Manager, Reality Labs Research

About me

I am a Human-Computer Interaction researcher with expertise in mixed-initiative interfaces, input and interaction techniques, the design of interactive help systems, and the usability of feature-rich software.

My immediate background is in computer science, but I draw on a diverse set of skills, including user-centered design, rapid prototyping of interactive systems, and qualitative and qualitative study methods.

Employment history

  • Chatham Labs (2019–2020)
  • Autodesk Research (2015–2019)
  • Post-Doc @ University of Saskatchewan (2014–2015)
  • PhD @ University of Waterloo (2009–2014)
